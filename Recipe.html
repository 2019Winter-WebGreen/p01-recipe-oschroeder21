Steps To Making: Ground Beef Tacos

This web page is about how to make delicious ground beef tacos in ! easy step!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 package of tacos seasoning
  • Hard or soft taco shells
  • Optional: shredded cheese, lettuce, tomatos, sour cream

Directions:

  1. Put the meat in a pan on the stove
  2. Mix around the meat, and start chopping the meat into smaller pieces using the thing you are using to mix
  3. Do this until meat is brown and no longer has any red pieces
  4. Take it off the stove and tilt the pan so the meat is on one side and the grease is on the other
  5. Using 2 papertowels soak up the grease and throw it away
  6. Return it to the lit stove, pour the 1 cup of water, and 1 taco package in, and mix
  7. Wait until meat fully absorbs the water, and taco seasoning is mixed

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes


Now that you have your beef, feel free to add the optional things: shredded cheese, lettuce, tomatos, and sour cream!
